# Health Module --- Overview

Updated: 2026-03-04

The Health module manages **personal health, diet, workouts, and medical
records**.

Sub-modules:

Health Records\
Diet Tracking\
Workout / Training\
Medical Documents

The goal is to provide a **centralized health history system** where
users can track lifestyle habits and store important medical
information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Capabilities

Health metrics tracking\
Diet and nutrition logging\
Workout tracking\
Medical appointments history\
File storage for medical documents\
Health timeline

Example:

2026-03-02\
Doctor appointment → upload exam results

2026-03-03\
Workout session → log exercises

2026-03-04\
Diet log → track meals

------------------------------------------------------------------------
