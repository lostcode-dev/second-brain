# Finance Module --- Overview

Updated: 2026-03-04

The Finance module provides a complete **personal finance management
system**.

Capabilities:

Income management\
Expense tracking\
Debt tracking\
Recurring transactions\
Financial dashboard\
Net worth tracking

The module integrates with the **Life OS ecosystem**.

Possible connections:

Debt → Goal\
Debt → Task\
Debt → Habit

Example:

Debt: Pay credit card\
Linked goal: Become debt free\
Linked task: Pay installment\
Linked habit: Weekly finance review

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Concepts

### Income

Represents money entering the system.

Examples:

Salary\
Freelance work\
Investments\
Transfers

Income may be **recurring**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Expenses

Represents money leaving the system.

Examples:

Rent\
Food\
Transport\
Subscriptions

Expenses may also be recurring.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Debts

Tracks liabilities.

Examples:

Credit card\
Loans\
Installments

Debts may contain multiple installments.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Net Worth

Represents assets owned.

Examples:

Bank accounts\
Investments\
Crypto\
Savings

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Financial Categories

Examples:

Housing\
Food\
Transport\
Health\
Education\
Entertainment
